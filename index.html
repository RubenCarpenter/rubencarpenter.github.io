About me

I am an undergraduate at Yale studying mathematics. I am especially drawn to problems where combinatorial questions interact with geometry, algebra, probability, or dynamics.

I am also involved with the olympiad mathematics community through problem writing, teaching, and mentoring.

Research interests

Some themes I am currently interested in include additive combinatorics, geometric combinatorics, extremal problems, and enumerative questions arising from geometric structures.

A typical object I like thinking about is a sumset \[ A+B = \{a+b : a \in A,\ b \in B\}, \] and how the size or structure of such a set reflects the structure of the original sets.
